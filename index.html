FAQs

What is Space?+

Space is a lightweight app that lets your people know when you're available.

Is Space a messaging app?+

No. Space is the signal. Connection happens outside the app.

Why would I use it?+

Because staying connected isn't usually about caring. It's about timing. Space removes the initiation barrier and creates zero obligation. It's a porch light, not a knock on the door.

Who can see my Space?+

Only your Circle. You choose who they are.

What happens when my Space ends?+

It quietly disappears. No history. No pressure.

Is Space free?+

Yes. The core experience will always be free.

How do I get Space?+

Takes about 30 seconds. No app store required.
Open app.spaceapp.me in your phone's browser, tap the share icon, then Add to Home Screen.
