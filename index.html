Frequently asked questions

What is Space?+

Space is a lightweight app that lets you signal to your close circle: "I have space for connection right now." With one tap, your people get a soft notification. You control your availability duration and method.

Is Space a messaging app?+

No. Space is a signal to bridge connection. It tells your circle you're available.
Connection happens outside the app via call, video, or in person.

Why do I need this?+

Connection typically requires initiation or planning — which carries social weight. Space broadcasts softly to your whole circle at once, removes the initiation barrier, and creates zero obligation for anyone who receives it. It's a porch light, not a knock on the door.

Who sees when I turn on Space?+

Only the people that you've added to your circle. You can also filter visibility by groups you create — such as inner circle, family, or friends. You control your availability, for how long and what you're up for.

What happens when my space expires?+

Space turns off quietly. No notification, no announcement, no record.

Is Space free?+

Yes. The core signal — turning on Space and receiving notifications from your circle — will always be free. Space is independently built and funded by people who believe in it.

How do I get Space on my phone?+

Takes about 30 seconds. No app store required.
Open app.spaceapp.me in your phone's browser
Tap three dots → Share → Add to Home Screen
